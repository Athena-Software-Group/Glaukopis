Why CC0 and not BSD-2-Clause for the row tag:

  redistribution. CC0 has no such requirement. Tagging rows as
  "misp-galaxy-cc0" lets the v13 licence-allowlist gate
  (tmpl_gen/scripts/check_corpus_licences.py) accept them without the
  consumer needing to bundle the BSD copyright header. The vendored
  LICENSE.md remains in this directory so the BSD branch is also
  available if a downstream consumer prefers that path.
